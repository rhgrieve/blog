So why am I doing this? 

I'd be embarassed to tell you how many times I've tried to start writing, or putting myself out there publicly without success. I could give you a few reasons why that is likely to be the case, but I suspect for me it has always come down to a matter of identity. 

In the past, whenever I've attempted to publish myself online, I've always struggled to express myself in a way that feels authentic. Even sitting here now, I'm feeling very uncomfortable doing this. 

## the dangers of comparison

- Echo chambers, groupthink, dogma (i.e "celebrity" web developers vs. tech purists 
- Open source community, tons of knowledge
- Felt like i didn't fit into a specific bucket (I have a lot of interests.. too many)  

## a sense of identity

- Bounced through various mental health modalities (talk therapy, medication, etc.) 
- Realized I lacked a fundamental sense of identity
- Journey to: 
    - Rediscover my true interests
    - Document things more mindfully and intentionally
    - Take the time to get to know myself as a person and how I want to be represented online
