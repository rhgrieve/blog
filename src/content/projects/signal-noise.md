An experiment in minimal feed reading. The idea was: what if a feed reader showed you *less* instead of more? No previews, no images, no social proof. Just titles and dates.

Built it as a weekend project. Used it for a few months. Learned that the constraint was useful but too aggressive — I ended up wanting at least a one-line summary.

Archived, but the thinking behind it still influences how I approach information density in other projects.
